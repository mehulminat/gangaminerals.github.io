River Sand

River sand is made up of of rounded particles that may or may not contain clay or other contaminants.

Washed Construction Sand

The term 'washed' refers to the process of removing clay, silt, and dust from sand before it is allowed to drain. Washed sand relates to the procedure, thus it can be gritty or smooth, and it can be used for a variety of things.

FBC Boiler Bed Material

FBC boiler bed material is a Low Ferric - High Temperature - Aluminous Refractory product made from super heat duty refractory aggregates with moderate alumina content, a neutral chemical character, and a dense texture.

AFS Size Foundry Sand

Foundry sand is high quality silica sand that is a by-product from the production of both ferrous and non-ferrous metal casting industries.
