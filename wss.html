What is River Sand?

River sand is made up of of rounded particles that may or may not contain clay or other contaminants.

Where can you use it?

TBecause it is required for the manufacturing of concrete and cement, river sand is one of the most widely utilised building materials. River sand is a versatile building ingredient that is commonly used in the following applications:
1.Concrete mixes.
2.For the purpose of filling.
3.Before laying paving, it is necessary to give substance.

What is river sand used for?

It's a high-quality white-grey sand that's commonly utilised in concrete and masonry projects. It can also be used for plastering, bricklaying, and RCC, among other things. Because water is already contained inside its particles, this sand has a better grain form and a smooth texture, and it requires less moisture. River sand's softness makes it suitable for use in concrete and brickwork. Plastering, RCC, and a variety of other block works benefit from river sand.
